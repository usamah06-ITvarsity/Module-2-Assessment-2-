Mr Price is a popular South African retail company known for its affordable and trendy clothing, accessories, and home goods. Founded in 1985, the brand has grown rapidly, appealing to a wide audience with its fashionable yet budget-friendly offerings. Mr Price focuses on delivering value by offering the latest styles at competitive prices, making it a go-to destination for shoppers looking for quality products without breaking the bank. The company has expanded its footprint beyond South Africa, establishing a presence in several other African countries, and continues to evolve with the changing retail landscape.

Edgars is a well-established South African retail chain known for offering a wide range of clothing, footwear, cosmetics, and homeware. Founded in 1929, Edgars has built a reputation for providing quality products that cater to a diverse customer base, from everyday essentials to high-end fashion brands. The store is particularly recognized for its comprehensive selection of both local and international brands, making it a key player in the South African retail market. With a focus on delivering stylish and premium products, Edgars continues to be a preferred shopping destination for those seeking a blend of fashion and quality.
